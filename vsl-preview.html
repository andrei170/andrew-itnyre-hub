VSL Preview Script - Andrew Itnyre (@mastersroofing.memphis)

Phase 1 deliverable · Short VSL preview only. 90-120 seconds. Not a full script.

Format Note

This is a short preview VSL - 90-120 seconds on camera. Not an 8-minute full script. The purpose is to give page visitors a face-to-face intro to the Masters Method before they decide to apply. Swap this for a custom recorded VSL on Day 1 of the install.

Production Brief

Length: 90-120 seconds Format: Talking head. On camera, direct to lens. No heavy production. Trade setting preferred (job site, truck, shop). Tone: Direct, grounded, unpretentious. Trade voice. The same energy as a strong reel, not a sales presentation. No teleprompter feel. Bullet points only. Speak from knowledge, not from a script.

Script Outline (Bullet Points for Andrew to Riff From)

Opening - The credibility hook (20 seconds) - "I grew up in this trade. My dad built Masters Roofing Memphis." - "I run sales and marketing for the family company. We operate in Memphis." - "25,000 people follow @mastersroofing.memphis - a lot of them are contractors and operators like you."

The Gap (25 seconds) - "Here is what I kept seeing: other family roofing operators watching what we do and asking how we do it." - "And I had nothing to send them. No course, no system, no structured way to share it." - "So I built one."

What the Masters Method Is (30 seconds) - "Six modules. The actual systems behind Masters Roofing Memphis." - "How we handle sales conversations, how we manage the family dynamics, how we landed brand partnerships, how we built the brand alongside the local business." - "This is not theory. This is what we do."

Who It Is For (15 seconds) - "If you are running a family roofing business or trying to build one - this is for you." - "If you are a 2nd-gen roofer figuring out how to take it to the next level - this is for you." - "If you want to build a brand alongside the business, not just run jobs - this is for you."

Soft Close (10 seconds) - "Take four minutes to fill out the application below." - "I will follow up personally." - [End]

Day-1 Swap Note

This outline is a placeholder structure. On Day 1 of the install, Andrew records a genuine 90-120 second video using these bullet points as a loose guide. No teleprompter. Trade setting. Camera phone is fine. The authenticity is the point.

The page will go live with the recorded video in the hero section. Until then, the application form is the primary conversion event.

What NOT to Do in the VSL

  • No fake energy or forced enthusiasm
  • No claims Andrew cannot back with specifics
  • No hype language ("transform," "unlock," "game-changer")
  • No production tricks that make it feel like a polished sales video - the rawness is the brand
  • No minute CTAs ("book a 20-minute call") - route to the application form only
