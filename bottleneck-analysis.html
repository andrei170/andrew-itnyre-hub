Three Bottlenecks I Found Inside Your Funnel - Andrew Itnyre

All three install in less than 7 days from kickoff. Built to compound. Order by impact.

Snapshot

  • Masters Roofing Memphis = local family business (core operation)
  • 2nd-gen roofer with genuine trade authority and visible content momentum

Bottleneck #1 - No Info Product for the Operators Watching You

The asymmetry: Andrew is already doing what hundreds of other 2nd-gen roofers and family trade operators want to learn. The 25K @mastersroofing.memphis audience is not all Memphis homeowners looking for a roof. A meaningful portion are other contractors, trade operators, and family business owners watching the reels and wondering how Andrew runs sales, builds brand partnerships, and manages the family operation. Right now, they follow. There is nothing to buy.

A $297-$497 "Masters Method" self-paced course - the systems, sales process, and brand-building approach Andrew uses at Masters Roofing Memphis - gives those watching a place to spend $300-500 and turns a local trade IG into a national education brand for family roofing operators.

Why it's costing him: - 25K followers with zero conversion path for operators who want to learn = zero info product revenue - No mid-tier means the only way to "work with Andrew" is to hire Masters Roofing Memphis for a Memphis roof job - First-mover advantage in the family/2nd-gen roofing operator niche disappears the moment someone else builds it first

The fix: - "Masters Method: How We Run a Family Roofing Operation" - 6-module self-paced course - Modules: 2nd-gen handoff and family dynamics, sales process, brand partnerships and sponsorships, team management, marketing on a trade budget, building a reputation in a local market - $297 standard, $497 with a 30-min Q&A call with Andrew - Hosted on Skool or Kajabi, sold off a landing page

Estimated impact: Year-1 floor $30-80K/yr at 100-200 buyers x $297-$497. Conservative at 25K audience. Doubles with audience growth.

Why this is #1: Highest ceiling relative to what Andrew already has. Zero competition in the family/2nd-gen roofing operator lane. The asset (lived experience + content) already exists. The monetization layer is what's missing.

Bottleneck #2 - No Lead Magnet or Scorecard Between IG and a Sale

The leak: The 25K @mastersroofing.memphis audience has nowhere to opt in. They can call Masters Roofing Memphis (only works if they are in Memphis). They can follow. That's it. There is no quiz, no scorecard, no free PDF, no email capture. Every person who watches a reel and thinks "I want to run my family roofing business like this" walks away with no way to stay in Andrew's orbit.

A "Family Roofing Operator Scorecard" - 8 questions, returns a 0-100 score for family trade operations, captures email, segments by business stage - converts the casual IG follower into a tracked email subscriber Andrew can market to indefinitely.

Why it's costing him: - 10-15% IG-to-email is achievable in trade with the right magnet = 2,500-3,750 incremental email subscribers from existing IG audience - Without an email list, Andrew cannot pre-sell a course, promote brand partnerships, or build a recurring audience relationship outside of the algorithm - No segmentation means no ability to identify which followers are operators (buyers) versus homeowners (non-buyers)

The fix: - "Family Roofing Operator Scorecard" - 8-question quiz on ScoreApp or Typeform - Returns a custom score and 3-action plan based on stage (starting out, established local, scaling) - Segments by: years in trade, team size, revenue band, biggest operational bottleneck - Triggers an 8-touch nurture sequence routing to the Masters Method course

Estimated impact: Year-1 lift $15-40K/yr incremental from email-driven course sales. Compounds for life of the list.

Why this is #2: Foundation for everything else. Fast install (less than 7 days). Email list is the asset every other bottleneck rides on. Small investment, permanent compounding return.

Bottleneck #3 - Brand Partnerships Have No Audience Monetization Layer

The contradiction: Andrew has visible brand partnerships - sponsorships and partnerships observable from public surfaces. These prove that brands see value in the @mastersroofing.memphis audience. But Andrew is monetizing those brand partnerships at whatever the one-time deal value is, with no infrastructure to compound the audience value back to him directly.

An email list + a productized offer turns brand partnerships from one-time transactions into proof of audience value. Andrew can charge more per partnership when he can show email subscribers, open rates, and an engaged operator audience. He can also create affiliate arrangements with brand partners for the Masters Method course (partners promote to their own audiences, Andrew splits the revenue).

Why it's costing him: - Brand partnerships without an email list = underpowered. Brands pay for access to audiences, not just followers. - No affiliate layer means brand partner audiences (which may be large) never see the Masters Method offer - Andrew's audience leverage increases dramatically once he has an email list to show alongside the IG follower count

The fix: - Email list + audience stats dashboard (Beehiiv or ConvertKit) shows brand partners the full audience picture - "Masters Method" course creates an affiliate opportunity: brand partners promote the course to their audiences, Andrew splits the revenue - Branded sponsor integration in the email nurture sequence (done right, increases both sponsor value and subscriber value)

Estimated impact: 20-40% uplift on brand partnership deal value + affiliate revenue layer. Compound return once email list is in place.

Why this is #3: Multiplier on existing relationships Andrew already has. Does not require new audience acquisition. Installs on top of #1 and #2.
