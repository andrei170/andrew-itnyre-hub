Funnel Blueprint - Andrew Itnyre (@mastersroofing.memphis)

Architecture deliverable · Full funnel diagram, phase mapping, tech stack, integration points.

Funnel Overview

Entry point: @mastersroofing.memphis IG (25K) via reels and bio link Capture: Family Roofing Operator Scorecard (quiz, email opt-in) Convert: Masters Method course ($297-$497) Scale: Brand partnership amplification + email list monetization Future high-tier: Group coaching or mastermind for family trade operators

Phase Map

Phase 1 - Core Funnel Install (Days 1-3)

What ships: - Family Roofing Operator Scorecard live on ScoreApp or Typeform - Bio link updated to route to scorecard as primary CTA - Masters Method landing page live (HTML, mobile-responsive) - Email platform connected (ConvertKit or Beehiiv) - Welcome email triggered on scorecard completion

Tech: - ScoreApp (quiz) or Typeform - ConvertKit or Beehiiv (email) - Stripe or Whop (payment) - Skool or Kajabi (course hosting)

Output: Scorecard is live. Email capture is running. Payment is ready.

Phase 2 - Nurture Layer (Days 4-7)

What ships: - 8-touch email nurture sequence active - Segmentation tags configured (operators vs non-operators vs homeowners) - Course pre-sell sequence added to nurture tail - Bio link router configured (scorecard primary, course secondary)

Tech: - Email platform automations (ConvertKit sequences or Beehiiv broadcast) - Zapier connection between Typeform/ScoreApp and email platform

Output: Every new scorecard completion enters the nurture automatically. No manual work.

Phase 3 - Content Compound (Post-Install, Ongoing)

What happens: - 30-hook content calendar rolls into reel production - 1 reel per month specifically drives to scorecard - Brand partnership content integrated at 1-in-5 ratio - Email broadcast cadence: 1-2x per week to growing list

Output: Compounding subscriber growth and brand partnership amplification.

Phase 4 - Paid Traffic (Post-Install, When Ready)

What happens: - Pixel and GA4 installed on landing page and scorecard - Top-performing organic reels boosted on Meta - Retargeting campaigns for scorecard starts and landing page visits - Lookalike audiences built from email subscribers

Output: Paid acquisition layer amplifies organic content momentum.

Integration Points

  • Scorecard to Email: Zapier or native ScoreApp integration pushes email + score to ConvertKit/Beehiiv tag
  • Payment to Course Access: Stripe/Whop webhook triggers Skool/Kajabi access grant
  • Email to Brand Metrics: ConvertKit/Beehiiv stats exported monthly for brand partnership reporting
  • IG to Scorecard: Bio link + 1 monthly reel explicitly routing to scorecard

Day-1 Gaps to Fix

  1. Bio link currently routes to local service website only - needs scorecard as primary CTA
  2. No email capture anywhere in the current digital presence
  3. No payment infrastructure for info products
  4. Course hosting platform needs to be selected and configured

All four fix in Phase 1 (Days 1-3).

What Does Not Change

  • Masters Roofing Memphis continues as the core local business - the info product is additive
  • @mastersroofing.memphis brand and content style stay intact - the scorecard and course extend it, not replace it
  • Brand partnerships continue on current terms - the email list makes them more valuable over time
  • Andrew stays the operator and face - AG1 builds the infrastructure, not the content
